Biomedical Engineering: My Focus

Biomedical Engineering represents the convergence of engineering and medicine. My passion lies in developing technological solutions to improve patient diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation.

My key areas of interest include:

  • **Bioinstrumentation and Medical Devices:** Design and optimization of sensors and equipment for monitoring and therapy.
  • **Biological Signal Processing (ECG, EEG, EMG):** Application of algorithms to extract relevant clinical information.
  • **Biomechanics and Materials:** Study of human movement and development of innovative prosthetics/orthotics.
  • **Health Informatics (eHealth/Telemedicine):** Implementation of information systems for managing health data.
